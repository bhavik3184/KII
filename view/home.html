About KRISH INDIA INTERNATIONAL

Krish India International is one of the leading Manufacturer - Exporter of Agricultural Machinery and Implements world-wide. A reputed name for quality products, KRISH has expanded its product range to cater to their customers ever increasing demands.

Established in India in the year 2008, KRISH has earned its name as one of the reliable Exporter from India with exclusive sourcing arrangements for some of the leading clients from African Continent.

KRISH comprises of a young and Dynamic team fully supported by professionals in International Business for over three Decades with complete focus on offering Quality products at competitive prices to our customers / clients. The expanded product range has been an addition to its reputation.

The spare parts and component division for Engines and Pumps has been one of the key support to the business development and KRISH can supply critical components and replacement parts for all types of Diesel Engines at very reasonable price without compromising on Quality. We are also Importers of components / electrical products for our prestigious customers in India.

A glance through Product Range would be sufficient testimony to our claims and the list could be endless.

Our young team at KRISH is always keen to increase and serve our clientele for complete guidance and satisfaction for supplies from India as well as from the third countries through their associate manufacturers.
